What does Teachers offer teachers?

We offer a platform for teachers to help students online and via live chat. This includes:

  • Access to our community of learners
  • Secure online payments
  • Integrated video chat platform and messaging
  • Responsive support via chat, email and phone

Who can teach?

Teacher does require formal teaching credentials. We do require criminal background check before you start teaching. Teacher must be citizens of or resident in the US, Philippines, Canada, Australia or New Zeland.

What does it cost? And how do I get paid?

It's free to signup on Teacher. We take a 30% service fee per transaction, so we only get paid when we're successful in bringing you customers. You receive payment via Paypal every two weeks.
